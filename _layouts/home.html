I’m Andrew Munroe, an award-winning digital producer and writer based in Vancouver.

About me

I develop, design, manage, and write for digital projects — mostly things related to journalism, non-profits, and education. You can say hello via email, LinkedIn, or GitHub.

Recent work

The House that Plastic Built

Writing & Design & Development & Data Viz

An interactive feature story I wrote, designed, and developed on the history of subsidies to the plastics industry. Co-produced by NBC News. Nominated for the 2022 DPA for Best Science and Technology Storytelling.

The Big Melt

Art direction & Design & Development & Data Viz

This is the story of Klinaklini, one of the largest glaciers in North America, and the impacts of its accelerating melt rate. It was produced as an interactive feature with the Tyee and nominated for the 2022 DPA for Best Editorial Package.

Stanley Park Ecology Society

Design & Development & Strategy

A custom-made WordPress theme for SPES. This incorporated donation management and ticket sales through the Zeffy fundraising platform, event scheduling, and CRM integration via Zapier — with ease of use and maintainability as top priorities.

Disability Tax Credit Tool

Design & Development & Strategy

A Vue.js based tool for the Disability Alliance BC to simplify applying for federal tax credits. This tool was refined based on my own testing with NVDA, and in-person beta test feedback from people who use assistive technology.

Writing Samples

Writing & Design

This is a selection of writing samples and design documentation from projects ranging from an interactive Mars Rover exhibit at the HR MacMillan Space Centre to a satirical apocalypse training games

America’s Medical Supply Crisis

Art Direction & Design & Development

A project in collaboration with AP News and the PBS series FRONTLINE. Done using SVG images and simple vanilla JS/CSS based animations. Nominated for a DPA for Best Digital Design.

IJB at the University of Toronto’s DLSPH

Design & Development & Strategy

The IJB is a new unit at the Dalla Lana School of Public Health that focuses on collaborative investigative journalism and training the next generation of reporters. I designed and developed a WordPress theme for their launch.

The Fish You (Don't Know You) Eat

Art Direction & Design & Development

In collaboration with NBC News, this project looked at the hidden costs of the global fishmeal industry in China, Peru and West Africa. Won the 2020 DPA for Best Digital Editorial Package.

Turning Points

Art Direction & Design & Development

The landing page for an eight-part series on alcohol use, addiction, and healing in Yellowknife. Each piece was directed by the storytellers themselves, and aired on PBS NewsHour. Uses the Vimeo API for custom video loading.

KitPrep

Design & Development

This was a demo project created with React and Firebase that allows people to create, save, and share custom emergency preparedness checklists (without needing to create any new accounts).
