I’m Andrew Munroe, an award-winning digital producer and writer based in Vancouver.

About me

I develop, design, manage, and manage for digital projects — mostly things related to journalism, non-profits, and education. You can say hello via email, LinkedIn, or GitHub.

Recent work

Stanley Park Ecology Society

Design & Development & Strategy

A custom-made WordPress theme for SPES. This incorporated donation management and ticket sales through the Zeffy fundraising platform, event scheduling, and CRM integration via Zapier — with ease of use and maintainability as top priorities.

The Big Melt

Art direction & Design & Development & Data Viz

This is the story of Klinaklini, one of the largest glaciers in North America, and the impacts of its accelerating melt rate. It was produced as an interactive feature with the Tyee and nominated for the 2022 DPA for Best Editorial Package.

Disability Tax Credit Tool

Design & Development & Strategy

A Vue.js based tool for the Disability Alliance BC to simplify applying for federal tax credits. This tool was refined based on my own testing with NVDA, and in-person beta test feedback from people who use assistive technology.

America’s Medical Supply Crisis

Art Direction & Design & Development

A project in collaboration with AP News and the PBS series FRONTLINE. I using SVG images and simple vanilla JS/CSS based animations. Nominated for a DPA for Best Digital Design.

The House that Plastic Built

Writing & Design & Development & Data Viz

An interactive feature story on the history of subsidies to the plastics industry. Co-produced by NBC News. Nominated for the 2022 DPA’s Best Science and Technology Storytelling.
